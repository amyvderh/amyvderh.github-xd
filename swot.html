My SWOT-analysis

On this blogpost you will read about my SWOT-analysis. SWOT stands for Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities and Threats. It's handy to make a SWOT-analysis for yourself to get an overview of what you're good at and what you need to look out for.

Strengths:
  • Helpful
  • Critical
  • Ambitious when interested
Weaknesses
  • Quickly stressed
  • Lack in experience in programming
  • Lack in knowledge about the ICT- industry
Opportunities:
  • Chance for selfdevelopment/confidence
  • A good job
Threats:
  • Lack of experience influences my chances of succeeding
  • Not being accepted, because girls is a minority group in ICT
