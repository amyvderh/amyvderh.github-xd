FAQ

If you have certain questions about how some things work at the HZ, here is an overview of questions you might have

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ? A: Step 1: Put money on your HZ card. Step 2: Send your document to a printer in school. Step 3: Go to a printer in the schoolbuilding. Step 4: Scan your card and pick the right document to print.

2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ? A: Step 1: Put money on your card. Step 2: Go to a printer in the schoolbuilding. Step 3: Scan your card and you will be able to send the document to your email on your laptop.

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop? A: Step 1: Go to HZ webshop. Step 2: Log in, add a product to your basket and pay.

4. Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID? A: Step 1: Visit the website HZ website Step 2: Click on current affair and you will see the topic: Information about the coronavirus. The newest updates will be available there.

5. How can you book a project space in one of the wings? A: Step 1: Search up Spacereservation Step 2: Once you arrive at the new page, click on "make a reservation"

6. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot? A: Step 1: Go to HZ website. Step 2: After that, click on about HZ and click the location "Middelburg". Step 3: Click the topic "parking". There you will find the intructions.
